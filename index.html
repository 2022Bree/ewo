Eagle wings organization is a youth led and focused community based organization based in Kenya. The organization was registered in 2020 and currently working in Kisumu county. Our aim is to mobilize and capacity build voices of the community in identification of the social needs of women and youths from vulnerable communities through leadership, governance, skills development and economic empowerment.

Our wealth as an organization lies in volunteers (young people) and the community that is why we invest in empowering our community with knowledge. We uphold a culture of humanity, volunteerism,collaboration and creativity .

Our Purpose of Existence

The Philosophy

"Unease head puts on the crown"

People who work or volunteer with Eagle Wings are enthusiastic in making use of their skills to improve the lives of other people. The philosophy drives the organization into shaping individuals, especially young people to be servants to humanity. Eagle Wings drives its energy from thinking as volunteers and making the priorities of the vulnerable and marginalized the priorities of the organization. This is the belief for our existence.

Mission

“Economically Build capacities of communities to promote inclusive and empowered society”

Wings utilizes an empowering approach throughout all its work with a focus to enabling individuals and institutions to effectively design, implement, monitor and evaluate their own successes in addressing the social determinants of health.

Vission

“An inclusive, economically empowered Community living in a peaceful environment”

The organization addresses the conditions that affect the well-being of individuals (social determinants of health) with the belief that no one is left behind in promoting change.
